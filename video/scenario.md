## Recording Tips

- **Software**: OBS Studio (free) or Loom
- **Layout**: split-screen terminal (left) + browser/VS Code (right)
- **Terminal font**: 16pt minimum, dark theme
- **Speed**: don't rush the orchestrator scene — narrate while it runs
- **Pauses**: pause 2-3 sec on key artifacts for readability
- **Browser**: zoom to 125% for better visibility in recording
- **Length**: aim for 8-10 minutes, cut dead time in post-production
